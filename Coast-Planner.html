Before you book: each park card links to its official state parks page with a reservation button. For Oregon parks, call 800-452-5687; for Washington parks, call (888) 226-7688. Yurts are popular and fill up fast — some (like Beverly Beach) get reserved up to nine months out. Rustic yurts run roughly $42–$62/night plus lodging tax; deluxe yurts (private bath, kitchenette) run $81–$99. Bring your own bedding — beds have vinyl mattresses only, and cooking indoors is limited to deluxe units with a provided microwave. Washington's coastal parks also require a Discover Pass for day-use vehicle entry (not needed if you're staying overnight in a yurt).
